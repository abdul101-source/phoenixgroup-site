Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Varix Intelligence implementation and value.

We already use Procore/Asite. Why do we need Varix?
Procore and Asite are excellent systems of record—they organize your documents beautifully. Varix is a system of intelligence that reads what those tools store and acts before value leaks. When a drawing revision gets uploaded to Procore, Varix compares it to the previous version and flags scope changes automatically.
Our team is stretched thin. Do we have time to implement this?
This is the exact reason Varix exists. Implementation requires about 8-10 hours from your commercial team over 30 days. Within 60 days, Varix typically saves 15+ hours per week in manual admin work. The time investment pays back within 6 weeks.
What if our senior QS leaves during implementation?
This is actually Varix's most powerful use case. Every interaction, successful argument, and client behavior pattern is captured in the system. When staff leave, their replacement inherits complete client intelligence, historical strategies, and evidence templates. Instead of a 6-month learning curve, new QSs are effective in 6 weeks.
How secure is our commercial data?
Bank-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit. UK-based servers (AWS London). Your data never trains AI models accessible to others. We can provide detailed security documentation and facilitate InfoSec review with your IT team.

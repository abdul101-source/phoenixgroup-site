Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do I receive my cashback?
Most cashback is processed instantly to your M-Pesa account. In some cases, it may take up to 7 days depending on the merchant's confirmation process.
Is there a fee to use Ziada?
No, Ziada is completely free for shoppers. We earn a commission from merchants when you make a purchase, and we share a portion of that with you.
Which stores work with Ziada?
We partner with major retailers like Jumia, Carrefour, Naivas, as well as local cafés, travel agencies, and more. The list is constantly growing!
Do I need to install an app?
You can use Ziada through our mobile-friendly website, but we recommend downloading our app for the best experience and push notifications about new deals.

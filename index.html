AI QR network for Kenya

Ziada deploys smart QRs in malls, offices, estates, and even with “human pods”. Each QR auto-routes to the right deal:

  • Mobile scan: show best local offer or highest-paying partner.
  • Desktop scan: forward to official partner / affiliate site, still tracked.
  • Per influencer: each creator gets a QR and link → all payouts logged → M-Pesa weekly.

How the money flows

Brand to Ziada

Typical commission: 6–12% of the sale.

Ziada to customer

We pay out 50–70% of that to the buyer’s M-Pesa. Example: brand pays 10% → buyer gets 5–7%.

Ziada + franchise

The remaining 30–50% is Ziada revenue, and a share goes to the operator who placed the QR.

Businesses see repeat revenue. Users see real cash. Nobody pays upfront.

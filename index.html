Why Ziada Exists

Ziada was founded by Abdul Khadir Abdullahi, MRICS — a Chartered Quantity Surveyor who has managed major projects in the UK, Europe, and East Africa. After years of success in the built environment, Abdul wanted to create something that gives back — a system where people earn fairly, merchants grow honestly, and communities benefit together.

Guided by MRICS ethics of transparency and fiduciary care, Ziada is more than a cashback platform — it’s a bridge for social mobility, designed so everyday buyers gain from the value they create.

“I’ve made money building projects. Now I’m building something that gives it back.”
— Abdul Khadir Abdullahi MRICS, Founder

For Brands & Retailers

Partner with Ziada to offer verified cashback to customers. We manage the tech, tracking, and payouts — you only pay on confirmed sales. Build customer loyalty through transparency, not gimmicks.

  • CSR-ready: your sales directly empower communities.
  • Zero upfront cost; commission-based model.
  • Clear audit trail with MRICS-backed governance.

How It Works

  1. You shop at a partner store or brand.
  2. The transaction is verified via Ziada tracking.
  3. Ziada sends cashback directly to your M-Pesa wallet.

Transparent. Simple. Designed to make every shilling count.

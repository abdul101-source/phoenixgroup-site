Transparent & Fair Pricing

We believe in complete honesty about how your cashback works

Our Simple 50/50 Model

For every KSh 100 we earn from partner stores, we split it fairly after covering essential costs.

Example: KSh 1,000 Purchase

Store Commission (10%) KSh 100
Essential Fees (M-Pesa, Operations) - KSh 10
Net Amount to Split KSh 90
Your Cashback (50%) KSh 45
Ziada Platform (50%) KSh 45

Why This Works

  • Sustainable: We can continue serving you long-term
  • Transparent: No hidden fees or complicated terms
  • Fair: Equal sharing ensures mutual benefit
  • Growing: As we scale, your benefits increase
